Bio

The Story

Kelly Price is a contemporary African artist on SPACEBOYS records, known for a genre-blending approach that fuses traditional influences with forward-thinking production.

His work explores identity, ambition, and personal growth — delivered with the warmth and rhythm that define the new wave of South African contemporary music.
